Grand Rapids

World of Winter

Grand Rapids is host to the World of Winter! It is a vibrant celebration that transforms the city into a winter wonderland. This annual festival, held in the heart of the city, showcases the beauty and magic of the winter season. Visitors and locals alike are treated to a dazzling array of events and activities, including dazzling light displays, ice sculptures, and interactive art installations. The festival embraces the cold with open arms, offering opportunities for outdoor adventures like ice skating, snowshoeing, and even a whimsical human ice chess match. Grand Rapids truly comes alive during the World of Winter, fostering a sense of community and joy as people gather to revel in the enchanting atmosphere and embrace the beauty of winter in West Michigan.
